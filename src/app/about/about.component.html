The Rules of Acquisition are a collections of proverbs of the utlra-capatalist race know as the Frengi. The Frengi are a race of humanoids who inhabit the Star Trek Galaxy.

These rules are the capatalist law that defines the behavior of their civilization. All Frengi are expected to follow them. Deviation is unaccepatable.

While the Frengi first appeared in Star Trek: The Next Generation, the rules themselves did not appear until Star Trek: Deep Space Nine. Many episodes reference the rules; however, the list remains incomplete, even with supplemental material provided by other sources.
